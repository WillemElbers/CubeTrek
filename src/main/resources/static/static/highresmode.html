CubeTrek's Replay Mode

The experimental Replay Mode let's you relive and share your activity in an interactive 3D mode.
Replay mode makes use of Google Earth's 3D model, as presented at Google's I/O 2023 event. Replay your hike/run/bike/mountainneering adventure, zoom into the terrain, scroll around.

Please note: this is experimental, free, and still full of bugs (I assume).
As this relies on Google Map data, access is limited depending on available quotas.
Have fun.

Upload your own GPS Tracks

  • Step 1: Get a CubeTrek account

    Register for a new account. It's free, and you only need to provide a username and email address.

  • Step 2: Get your Activity Files

    CubeTrek accepts FIT files (e.g. from Garmin, Polar, Coros, Suunto devices) and GPX files.

    See here for instructions on how to download all your files from your Strava or Garmin account (bulk download).

    You can also connect your Garmin or Polar account to CubeTrek to automatically sync from your device.
    Please note: this will only sync future activities, past activities need to be uploaded to CubeTrek manually.

  • Step 3: Upload your files

    Login to CubeTrek and upload your files, one by one or upload an entire ZIP archive.

  • Done

    Open your track and click on the well hidden 3D Replay button to the bottom left of the classic 3D view.
